RED SOIL

Crops grown in red soil are

  1. cotton
  2. wheat
  3. pulses
  4. millet
  5. potatoes
  6. linseed
  7. jowar
*Commonly used Organic fertilizer in the red soil region are green manure,farmyard manure and crop residue

Cow dung isBEST ORGANIC FERTILIZER

*The best inorganic fertilizer for plants grown in red soil will be one with higher levels of potassium and phosphorus than nitrogen

Range for inorganic fertilizer is 50, 25, and 12 kg per acre
